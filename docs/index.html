How Signal scores — the signals explained

        
Every candidate gets a 0–100 composite. Thesis-fit dominates (~60%); the quality signals corroborate (~40%). Each signal is reputation-weighted and shown in the breakdown, so a human can see why. Scores triage — a partner decides.


        

          score = fit (dominant) + traction + credibility + signal strength( code_health · onchain · social · team · pre_public )
        


        
The base — is it on-thesis?

        

          
fit

          
Measures how well the company matches the fund mandate in config/thesis.yaml (themes & weights).

          
Why it's the gate — a strong, hyped company that's off-thesis still ranks low.

        

        

          
traction

          
Measures GitHub star velocity, log-dampened.

          
Kept honest dampened so a single spike can't dominate the score.

        

        

          
credibility

          
Measures an anti-gaming adjustment (penalty).

          
Kept honest anti-signals (meme coin, presale, 1000x) and buzzword-stuffing get screened out.

        


        
The signals


        

          
code_healthfree

          
Measures commit-velocity acceleration + how evenly work is spread across contributors (Gini).

          
Why teams accelerating with a real team (not one hero dev) are ~3.4× more likely to raise a Series A within 60 days.

          
Kept honest multiplicative — high velocity from a single dev scores near zero.

        


        

          
onchainfree · DefiLlamapremium · Nansen

          
Measures external stablecoin inflows, "real" TVL (revenue − incentives), holder distribution.

          
Why real dollars arriving + sticky, non-incentivised usage signal genuine demand before the token moves.

          
Premium · Nansen upgrades onchain with Smart-Money net inflow + holder share, and adds an on-chain smart-money convergence twin of network_radar (which credible wallets newly buy).

          
Kept honest subtracts emissions and tests retention, so rented "mercenary" liquidity earns nothing.

        


        

          
socialfree

          
Measures smart-follower convergence (credible builders/VCs following early) + OpenRank reputation, gated by Neynar's quality score.

          
Why you can't buy a respected founder's follow — early convergence front-runs undisclosed diligence.

          
Kept honest reputation-weighted and age-gated, so bot followers and fresh accounts don't move it.

        


        

          
teamfree · GitHubpremium · Harmonic

          
Measures free: technical-founder presence, team size, frontier-lab pedigree (GitHub). premium: prior exits + size, talent-flow (Harmonic).

          
Why founder quality is the most predictive signal — repeat, previously-exited, and technical teams win more.

          
Kept honest trust events (commits, exits) over self-asserted titles.

        


        

          
pre_publicfreepremium · Evertrace

          
Measures ecosystem grants (EF / Optimism), hackathon wins that kept building, research with an affiliation exit, recent formation.

          
Why ecosystem-vetted, pre-equity proof — catches teams 6–18 months before a round.

          
Kept honest retroactive grants > proposal grants; a hackathon counts only if the team kept shipping.

        


        

          
stage-gatepremium · Messari

          
Measures latest funding round, stage, and lead investors (Messari).

          
Why down-ranks already-funded companies (Series B/C+) so the pre-seed funnel isn't clogged; a tier-1 lead investor is light positive corroboration.

          
Kept honest a hard, legitimate signal (a funding fact) — distinct from the soft "early-stage" risks, which never penalise a good-fit company.

        


        
Guiding principle: raw counts are worthless — reputation-weight every interaction (so bot swarms self-cancel), prefer hard-to-fake "expensive" actions over cheap ones, gate on account age, and score ratios, not totals. Full research: docs/Sourcing_Signals_Research.md.
